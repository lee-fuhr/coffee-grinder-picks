How we choose our picks

We don’t run lab tests or time trials. Instead, we spend months reading owner forums, talking to coffee professionals, and cross-referencing published expert reviews from respected sources like Specialty Coffee Association members and veteran home-barista communities. We look for patterns: which models hold up after a year of daily use, which parts break first, and which companies support their products with real customer service. Our picks favor grinders with proven reliability, easy access to replacement burrs and parts, and a reputation for consistent particle distribution across common brew methods. We avoid flash-in-the-pan hype and instead highlight machines that have earned their place in kitchens and coffee shops over the long haul.
