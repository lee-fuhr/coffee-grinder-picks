Frequently asked questions

What is the main advantage of an electric burr grinder?

Convenience and consistency at volume. Press a button and walk away while it delivers an even grind, which makes it the easy daily driver for households brewing multiple cups every morning.

How do I keep an electric grinder consistent over time?

Grind fresh, keep the burrs clean, and empty the hopper of stale beans. Oils and fines build up and shift the grind, so an occasional clean and a burr brush keep results steady.

Do electric grinders make a mess with static?

Some do, fines cling and scatter when you remove the bin. A light spritz of water on the beans before grinding (the Ross Droplet Technique) tames static dramatically on most grinders.

Are stepped or stepless adjustments better for filter?

For filter coffee, stepped adjustment with enough steps is perfectly fine and easier to repeat. Stepless mostly benefits espresso, where micro-adjustments matter more.

How loud are electric burr grinders?

It varies by motor and housing. Quality grinders run a brief, contained whir; cheaper ones can be harsher. If a quiet kitchen matters, owner feedback on noise is worth weighing before you buy.
